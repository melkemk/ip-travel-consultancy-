Canada

Canada is a country of natural beauty and cultural diversity, making it an ideal destination for any kind of traveler. Immerse yourself in the stunning landscapes of the Rocky Mountains, the Canadian Shield, and the boreal forests, where you can hike, camp, fish and enjoy the view of the wild. Experience the culture and history of Canada's vibrant cities, such as Toronto, Montreal, and Vancouver. Visit iconic landmarks, explore museums and art galleries, and indulge in the local cuisine. Discover the country's rich Indigenous culture and enjoy winter and summer sports activities. Canada has something to offer for every traveler, let us take you on a journey through this beautiful country and make your dream vacation a reality.


New Zealand

New Zealand is a land of stunning natural beauty, offering visitors a diverse range of landscapes to explore, from the snow-capped peaks of the Southern Alps to the sandy beaches of the Coromandel. Visitors can indulge in outdoor activities such as hiking, camping, and sightseeing and discover the unique native species such as the kiwi bird and tuatara reptile.


Australia

Australia is a country of diverse landscapes, natural wonders and vibrant culture. Visitors can discover the unique Australian wildlife by visiting places such as Kangaroo Island, the Great Barrier Reef, and the Daintree Rainforest, or relax on one of the famous white sandy beaches like Bondi or Bells. There are many iconic landmarks to visit such as Sydney Opera House and the Sydney Harbour Bridge, and visitors can explore the Indigenous culture at places such as the Uluru-Kata Tjuta National Park. Visitors can also indulge in a diverse range of cuisines from seafood, to traditional Australian BBQ or fusion Asian cuisine. From the bustling cities to the outback, Australia offers an unforgettable experience for all types of travelers.


Denmark

Denmark is a country with a rich history and culture. Known as the land of fairytales and Vikings, visitors can discover Denmark's rich cultural heritage by visiting historical sites such as the Viking ring fortress at Aggersborg, and the historic city of Roskilde. Visitors can also experience the Danish way of life by exploring the picturesque towns, biking along the picturesque countryside, and savoring the delicious local cuisine.


Germany

Germany is a country with a rich cultural heritage and a vibrant modern society. Visitors can explore historic cities like Berlin, Munich, and Hamburg and discover the country's rich history by visiting landmarks such as the Berlin Wall, the Brandenburg Gate, and the Zwinger Palace. Germany is also known for its diverse landscapes and natural beauty, from the Bavarian Alps in the south, to the rolling hills of the north, Visitors can also enjoy the country's famous cuisine and beer culture.


South Africa

South Africa is a country of unparalleled natural beauty and cultural diversity. From the beaches of the Garden Route to the big 5 wildlife of the Kruger National Park, South Africa offers visitors a wide range of experiences. Visitors can explore the diverse culture, history and customs of the different tribes that lives in South Africa, and discover the legacy of the Apartheid in cities such as Johannesburg and Cape Town.



United States

The United States is a vast and diverse country, offering visitors a wide range of experiences from the bustling cities to the stunning natural wonders. Visitors can explore the rich cultural heritage of cities like New York, San Francisco, and New Orleans, visit iconic landmarks such as the Statue of Liberty and Mount Rushmore, and take in the breathtaking scenery of national parks such as Yellowstone and Yosemite. The U.S also offers a diverse cuisine and entertainment options, from Broadway shows to theme parks, visitors will never be short of something to do.



United Kingdom

The United Kingdom is a country with a rich history and culture. Visitors can explore historic cities like London, Edinburgh, and Bath, and discover the country's rich history by visiting landmarks such as Buckingham Palace, Stonehenge, and the Tower of London. Visitors can also indulge in the famous English tea culture and discover the diverse regions and nationalities of the UK. The United Kingdom is also renowned for its music, theatre and art scene, making it a perfect destination for culture enthusiasts.



Portugal

Portugal is a country with a rich history and culture, and a diverse range of landscapes. Visitors can explore the historic cities of Lisbon and Porto, discover the rich heritage of the country by visiting landmarks such as Belem Tower, Jeronimos Monastery, and the Sagrada Familia. Visitors can also take a stroll along the cobblestone streets and indulge in the delicious traditional Portuguese cuisine or enjoy a glass of the famous Port wine. The Algarve region offers picturesque beaches and cliffs, while inland offers the beauty of the Serra da Estrela mountain range.
